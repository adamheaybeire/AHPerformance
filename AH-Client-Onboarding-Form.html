Consent & Data Protection
Your data is important. Please read the following carefully and confirm your consent.
🔒
How your data is handled. AH Performance operates in full compliance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. Your personal and health data is encrypted, stored securely, and never shared with third parties. You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data at any time.
Data Processing Consent
I, Adam Heayberd trading as AH Performance, need to collect and process your personal data (name, contact details, date of birth, occupation) in order to provide personal training services to you. This data is processed under the lawful basis of contractual necessity (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(b)).
I consent to AH Performance collecting and processing my personal data for the purposes of providing personal training services.
Health Data Consent (Special Category)
Your health information (medical history, injuries, medications, PAR-Q responses) is classified as "special category data" under UK GDPR and requires your explicit consent to process. This data is used solely to ensure your safety during training and to design appropriate exercise programmes.
I explicitly consent to AH Performance collecting and processing my health data for the purposes of safe and effective programme design. I understand this data will be stored securely and never shared with third parties without my permission.
Communication Consent
To deliver your programmes, check-in reminders, and coaching feedback, I need to contact you via email and/or the AH Performance app.
I consent to receiving programme updates, coaching communications, and check-in reminders from AH Performance via email and app notifications.
Progress Tracking Consent
To monitor your progress, I will record data including body weight, measurements, performance metrics, and check-in responses. Progress photos are optional and stored with the same level of security as all other data.
I consent to AH Performance recording and storing my progress data for the purposes of monitoring and adapting my training programme.
Your Rights
Under UK GDPR, you have the right to: access all data held about you; correct any inaccurate data; request deletion of your data; withdraw consent at any time; restrict processing of your data; and data portability — receive your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, contact adam@ahperformance.co.uk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO (Information Commissioner's Office) at ico.org.uk.
Declaration
I declare that the information I have provided is true and complete to the best of my knowledge. I understand that withholding relevant health information may affect the safety of my training. I will inform AH Performance of any changes to my health status.
